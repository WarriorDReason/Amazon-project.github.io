Amazon’s key benefits and features

  • You must register to buy on Amazon but this process is free.
  • The prices of goods are often lower than in high street stores, and the cost of delivery can be kept low or is free depending on the service you choose
  • You can save items you like to a 'wish list' so that you can buy them later
  • Amazon remembers your previous purchases and wish lists, and recommends other items specific to your interests.
